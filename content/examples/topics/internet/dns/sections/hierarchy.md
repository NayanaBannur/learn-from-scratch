Those labels form an upside-down tree. The **root** sits at the top, the TLDs (`com`, `org`, `net`, country codes like `uk`) hang beneath it, and each domain hangs beneath its TLD. [[cite:rfc1034]]

Reading a name right to left is walking this tree from the root down to the exact host.
